Who I am

Ever since I was young, I had a love for two things: Design and Technology. As a young child I have been drawing cartoons. After Highschool, I entered college as a Graphic Design student at Spokane Falls Community College. There, I learned the fundamentals of design and mastered Adobe software. After my journey as a Graphic Design, I decided to fully focus on Web Development. In 2019, I enrolled in Lambda School as a Full Stack Web Development student. In just a couple months time, I gained a solid understanding of HTML, CSS3, Javascript, and using the terminal with git. I alo had the opportunity to create a website for a real company, ETS inc. The company is still using my website to this day.
